How we enable digital transformation

#}

We enable digital transformation at Defra in two ways:

  1. We provide strategic form consultancy for select ALBs to understand how to design good digital forms, and how to change processes to make them work better for staff and the public.
  2. We built a powerful digital form builder called the Defra Form Designer that enables complex data collection, automation and integration with Defra systems.
