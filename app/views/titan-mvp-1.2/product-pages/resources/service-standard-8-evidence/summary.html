Summary

The Defra Forms Designer demonstrates strong evidence for Service Standard point 8 through specific, documented examples:

  1. Continued research and iteration - 17 rounds of user research, 77 research iterations across 32 features, systematic use of feedback, data, and metrics to inform improvements
  2. Iterating with time constraints - Methods established for rapid iteration, quick wins identified and implemented, prioritised improvements based on user impact
  3. Content review and maintenance - Processes in place to review and update content regularly, design history maintained and kept current, feature status tracked
  4. Prioritising improvements - Evidence-based decision making for resource allocation, team priorities aligned with user needs, clear framework for determining value

All evidence includes specific dates, feedback sources, actions taken, and measurable results, demonstrating a systematic approach to iterating and improving the service frequently.
