About the Defra Forms team

The Defra Forms project aims to tackle Defra Group's reliance on paper (document-based) forms. The Defra Forms team is a multidisciplinary team in the Digital, Data and Technology Services (DDTS) directorate.

We enable digital transformation at Defra in two ways.

  1. We provide strategic form consultancy for select ALBs to understand how to design good digital forms, and how to change processes to make them work better for staff and the public.
  2. We built a powerful digital form builder called the Defra Form Designer that enables complex data collection, automation and integration with Defra systems.

We've published several blogs about our work, including:

  • Transforming Defra together: the journey from paper to online forms
  • How we are leading a bold and innovative approach to online forms
  • Being assessed and assessing – a tale of two perspectives: Part one
