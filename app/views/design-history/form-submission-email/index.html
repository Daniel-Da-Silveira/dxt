From: Defra Forms
To: email address
Subject: Form submitted to Defra

We have your form

We received your form submission for 'Declaration of completion of cleansing and disinfection for TB in England' on Tuesday 1 April 2025 at 10:30am.

What happens next

Your declaration has been sent to the Animal and Plant Health Agency (APHA) who will store it on your record. You will need to make another declaration if more TB reactor animals are disclosed.

Get help

Call 03000 200 301 Monday to Friday between 8:30am to 5pm. Or email TB.Advice@apha.gov.uk. We aim to respond within 2 working days.
