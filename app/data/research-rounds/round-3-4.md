### Detailed Research Summary

#### Rounds 3 & 4 – June/July 2024

**Research conducted:** Remote usability testing (24–25 June & 1–2 July 2024)
**Participants:** 8 content designers across various teams (Farming, Cefas, Environment Agency, Nature, Forestry, Climate Change)

### Key insights and recommendations by section

#### File Upload - Public View with JavaScript

- **Insights:** Interactions and statuses clear; unclear why two documents were required.
- **Quotes:** "I'm just wondering why I'd need to upload up to two. Is 1 file enough?"
- **Recommendation:** Clarify reasons for multiple uploads.

#### File Upload - Public View without JavaScript

- **Insights:** Refresh page link frequently overlooked.
- **Quotes:** "So was this link here the whole time telling me I need? Yeah, I missed it."
- **Recommendation:** Improve visibility and clarity of refresh instructions.

#### Error Messages - Public View

- **Insights:** Clear but virus-related error messages need additional detail.
- **Quotes:** Participants expected clearer virus-related guidance and expressed anxiety about red error messages.
- **Recommendation:** Clarify virus status and provide less alarming error presentations.

#### Check Your Answers Page

- **Insights:** Clear interactions and content in Round 4.
- **Recommendation:** Maintain clarity for error checking.

#### Forms Library

- **Round 3 Insights:** Clear overall but lacked search and alphabetical sorting.

  - **Quotes:** "I think what would be weird is I can't search or sort it alphabetically."

- **Round 4 Insights:** Search functionality effective; advanced search and state filter require further investigation.

  - **Quotes:** "'Search' works very well – it meets what users need."

- **Recommendations:** Continue refining search functionality and sort/filter options.

#### Sort by Options

- **Insights:** Sorting options understandable but not universally useful.
- **Quotes:** "I personally don't know why I'd want to know which is oldest or newest."
- **Recommendation:** Evaluate usefulness further and consider streamlining options.

#### Creating a New Form

- **Insights:** Clear and effective; need to select multiple organizations still exists.
- **Recommendation:** Enable multi-organisation selection.

#### Form Overview

- **Insights:** Content and interaction clear; minor usability prompts observed.

#### Editing Draft - Add Page

- **Round 3 Insights:** Mixed familiarity with the concept of "one question per page."
- **Round 4 Insights:** New bullet point design improved clarity significantly.
- **Quotes:** "It was much clearer to all participants."
- **Recommendation:** Maintain clear guidance on page structuring, consider additional educational resources.

#### Editing Draft - Component Addition

- **Round 3 Insights:** Confusion around components from design system.
- **Round 4 Insights:** Simplified flow from "Add Page" directly to "Add Component" significantly improved clarity.
- **Quotes:** Participants found the new direct flow clearer.
- **Recommendation:** Maintain streamlined interaction and continue evaluating clarity of component labels.

#### File Upload Editing

- **Round 3 Insights:** Mixed views; confusion around "Short label" and content formatting.
- **Round 4 Insights:** Improved significantly by removing confusing labels and providing examples.
- **Quotes:** "The new design tested well; labels and copy were clear."
- **Recommendation:** Continue refining optional labels and formatting capabilities.

#### Visualisation and Icons

- **Insights:** Icon representing file upload clear and effective.
- **Quotes:** Participants clearly understood icons and interactions.

### Next Steps

- Refine wording and positioning for refresh actions and CTAs
- Improve autocomplete for author filters in forms library
- Further usability testing for sorting options
- Clarify email and instructional text across form editing and library interactions
- Implement realistic form scenarios for enhanced testing
- Conduct accessibility testing with voice recognition software (Dragon) on 8 July 2024.
